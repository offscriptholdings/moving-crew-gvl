About Reedy

Reedy started with a bad move. David got quoted one number and billed another, with his stuff already on the truck. Nothing about it was illegal. It was just how the industry works, and we didn't think it had to. So every Reedy quote comes with a written cap: if the job runs long, we cover it.

We're owner-operated and labor-only, the muscle without the franchise. We run our own crews, no subcontractors, one crew from the first box to the last, and we don't leave until it's placed right.
